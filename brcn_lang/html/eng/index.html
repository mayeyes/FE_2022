Nine+ Scenic spots

MORE

  • Daecheon Beach

    The only shell powder beach in Asia Festivals are held on the sandy beach throughout the year.

  • Jukdo Island (Sanghwawon)

    Jukdo Island is called a bamboo island as it is densely packed with bamboo trees.

  • Seongjusan Recreational Forest

    Beauty of the Charyeong Mountains Forest baths in the valleys in the beautiful forests of the Charyeong Mountain Range

  • Gaehwa Art Park

    A large art complex featuring an herb land, a music hall, Birim Park, and Mosan Art Gallery

  • Muchangpo Beach

    A place of the Miracle of Moses that allows visitors to enjoy a bath on the beach and in forests.

  • Oeyeondo Island

    Beautiful scenery of three mountaintops guarding the surrounding islands

  • Chungcheong Suyeongseong Fortress

    A fortress with stacked stone walls to defend against foreign enemies from the West Sea

  • Cold Wind Resort

    Natural wind from the abandoned mine gets cooler as the outside temperature rises

  • Boryeongho Lake

    A route for scenic driving while appreciating the beautiful landscape and blue water

  • Oseosan Mountain

    Waves of silver grass like a watercolor Beautiful glow of the setting sun seen from the top

Nine Delicious Dishes

MORE

  • Cheonbuk Roasted Oyster

    Oysters from Cheonbuk district grow slowly due to a difference of tidewater, but they are excellent in taste and nutrients

  • Sahyeon Grapes

    Sahyeon-ri, in Nampo-myeon, has land suitable for cultivating grapes. Their good flavor and taste is due to lots of sugar found in grapes, and they are popular with consumers.

  • Baby Octopus

    Its chewy texture is above description when eaten with sweet hot bean paste. Other applications are fried octopus, raw octopus, combined with added ingredients and octopus casserole.

  • Seasoned Stingray with Vegetables

    Fresh stingray caught off the West Coast with cucumber and sesame provide chewy textures with sour and sweet tastes, attracting many gourmets.

  • Blue Crab Stew

    Crab is a digestive material and provides a clean taste because it has low fat and abundant proteins, so it is good for growing children, weak or the elderly. Other applications are pickled crab and smothered crab.

  • Boryeong Natural Laver

    Made from top quality materials produced from the clean West Sea, it keeps crisp with a sweet flavor and taste long. Good laver has lots of protein and tastes good.

  • Pen Shell Cuisine

    Oysters from Cheonbuk district grow slowly due to a difference of tidewater, but they are excellent in taste and nutrients. Oyster roasteds on charcoal in the winter will make you forget about cold weather.

  • Grilled clams

    Fresh clams from Boryeong, such as scallops, pen shells, conchs, shrimp, cockles, and abalone, and various seafood directly grilled on a grill, offering a variety of fresh seafood.

  • Seafood kalguksu

    Various seafood adds sea scent and the fun of eating with chewy noodles. With its cool soup and clean taste, anyone of all ages can eat healthy and deliciously.
