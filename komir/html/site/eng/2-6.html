프린트

We implement international cooperation projects with leading mining countries to strengthen our role as a bridge for mineral resources development by private sector to secure critical minerals in need for national prosperity and contribute to sustainable mining in the world.

In addition, we make an effort to realize win-win model for mutual growth with the private sector based on mutual cooperation.

Networking with leading mining countries.

We strengthen our ability to implement cooperation projects with leading mining countries, such as Canada and Australia by establishing international partnership with relevant organizations in the way of joint statement of cooperation which includes exchange of information and human resources, joint workshops in the field of mining as a whole from exploration to mine rehabilitation.

Also, we support domestic companies to expand their business abroad by supporting their participation in international mining events, hosting seminars (webinars) and symposiums, etc.

Overseas Mineral Resources Development Association (ORE:ON)

The Overseas Mineral Resources Development Association, named as ORE:ON, was established in 2017 to provide a platform for information exchange, networking and development of collaboration projects among member companies in the field of mineral resources.

ORE:ON is made up of 3 divisions including mineral resources development division, trading division and R&D division, and as an administrator and a facilitator, ORE:ON hosts online and offline assemblies, seminars while operating a YouTube channel

Overseas Information Service

We collect and provide up-to-date resources sector information and prospective mining projects in resource rich countries through our overseas network of representative offices, liaison officials and correspondents. You can check the latest overseas information and trends at our Korea Resource Information System, KOMIS.

Overseas representative offices (China, Canada, Chile), liaison officials (Kazakhstan, South Africa), overseas correspondents (Indonesia, Peru, Russia, Hong Kong, 3 Provinces in Northeast China), Australia subsidiary, Mongolia subsidiary

ODA and Mining Industry Full-Cycle Consulting

We cooperate with domestic and foreign government organizations, private companies, and international organizations to carry out ODA (official development assistance) and consulting projects covering a full spectrum of mining life cycle. Also, we’ve been making every effort to establish and strengthen our mutually beneficial relationships with foreign governments and private companies in resource rich countries

<major projects>
  • Establishment of Master Plan and Capacity Building for Mine Reclamation Management in Mongolia (2014~2021, KOICA ODA): establishment of the master plan, development of the training program, mine reclamation pilot program, construction of the education center & supply of equipment, etc
  • Construction of Mine Reclamation Management Information System for Sustainable Mineral Resources Development in Peru (2015~2018, KOICA ODA): field survey on 21 closed mines, establishment of mine reclamation management information system, etc.
  • Analyzing the Status of Mine Hazard and Developing the Management System for Mine Reclamation in Indonesia (2014~2018, KOICA ODA): establishment of analysis center, construction of AMD(Acid Mine Drainage) treatment facility, forest restoration, damaged by mining activities, etc.
  • Consulting Service to Undertake Sampling and Develop Engineering Designs of In-situ Remediation of Households in Chowa, Kasanda, Makululu and Other Contaminated Hotspots with Lead Bearing Material in Kabwe, Zambia (2019~2021, World Bank) : investigation and remedial design for soil contamination with lead.
  • PMC(project management consulting) Service for the Project for Enhancing Water Quality Monitoring System to Achieve SDG (sustainable development goals) 6 in Pakistan (2020~2024, KOICA ODA) : publication of water-related administrative statistics report, establishment of SOP (standard operating procedure) for water quality analysis laboratory operation, development of water MIS (monitoring information system), capacity building training, etc.
KOMIR, Into the World!

KOMIR ventures out into the world to secure critical minerals to keep pace with the fourth industrial revolution era and contribute to the sustainable development of the mining industry to make a cleaner Earth.
