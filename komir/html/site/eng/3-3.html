Ethics Charter

The Korea Mine Rehabilitation and Mineral Resources Corporation is the proud corporation for the people that properly manages mine damage, nurtures and supports the mineral resource industry, promotes economic vitalization of the mine areas, and ensures the stable supply of mineral resources contributing to the development of the national economy.

We promote the corporate vision and core values with pride and confidence and seek to become a trusted specialized organization leading the development of mine areas and resource security.

Recognizing that transparent and fair ethical corporate culture is essential, we have created the ethics charter, integrating our resolutions as follows, and use this as the top priority for all employees.

  • We achieve our mission with creative thinking and the spirit of challenge while observing fundamental laws and regulations based on high ethical values and strive to practice transparent management through a fair and honest work process.
  • We provide customers with goods and services of the best quality, practice customer respect, and promote mutual prosperity.
  • We promote enhancing the corporation’s values and sustainable growth and development through fair competition and innovation activities and contribute to local development through coexistence and cooperation.
  • We respect each employee's personality and creativity, treat employees based on their competence and achievement, and strive to support employees for the improvement of quality of life and capacity building.
  • We observe domestic and international laws and regulations and international agreements, protect and preserve the environment, participate in activities of public interest as a member of a society, and contribute to the nation's development and society.

Human Rights Management Charter

Human Rights Management Charter of Korea Mine Rehabilitation and Mineral Resources Corporation

We seek to protect the human rights of all employees and those related throughout all management activities, respecting and practicing international human rights norms as a public corporation, which nurtures and supports systematic mine damage management and mineral resource industry while contributing to the promotion of the local and national economies and resource security.

Therefore, we declare the Human Rights Management Charter of the Korea Mine Rehabilitation and Mineral Resources Corporation as follows and assure its practice as the standards for action and value judgment that all employees should observe.

  • We support and respect international standards and norms that support values such as human rights, labor, environment, and anti-corruption.
  • We do not discriminate based on race, religion, disability, gender, place of birth, or political viewpoint. 
  • We ensure freedom of association and collective bargaining to protect the rights of all employees. 
  • We do not allow forced labor or child labor in any form. 
  • We create safe and hygienic work environments to guarantee industrial safety. 
  • We strive for mutual development with our partner companies and support their cooperation in human rights management practices. 
  • We respect and protect the human rights of the local people of the business activity area. 
  • We do our best to preserve the environment for a healthy and pleasant life for the people and future generations. 
  • We make efforts not to threaten the safety of the people in business implementation and protect personal information collected during work. 
  • We provide swift and appropriate rescue measures for human rights infringements during the project execution and spare no effort to prevent it in advance.
