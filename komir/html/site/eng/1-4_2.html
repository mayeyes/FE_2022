Management Philosophy

Since the foundation of the Korea Mine Rehabilitation and Mineral Resources Corporation, the management philosophy of the CEO has aimed to raise the corporate value, achieve the mission, and establishment of goals as an integrated organization.

Creating Sustainable Corporate Values based on Integration, Harmony and Communication

Expand the establishment and the re-establishment of the organization by promoting omni-directional communication channels; Build up a corporate culture where the community spirit is shared; Present the future development visions; Provide consistent and comprehensive public services throughout the entire mining cycle to create synergistic effects.

Management Policy

The CEO’s determination to establish stable operational measures of the integrated organization and to merge the corporate cultures of the organizations

  • Physical and Chemical of Dual Organizations Integration

    The concentration of organization-wide capacity to successfully establish support system for the entire mine rehabilitation and mining industry as a far-sighted national policy under the slogan “two starts, one future”

  • Synergy Effects of the Integrated Organization Harmony

    An innovative public organization, swiftly responding to changes in the mining environment, acting as an innovator in the national mining industry by generating synergy and leading national resource security and the development of mining areas

  • with Local Community and Stakeholders of the Mining Industry Communication

    Creating an environment where the mining and abandoned mining areas grow self-sufficient economies through co-existence and cooperation, establishing a sustainable virtuous cycle through communication with the stakeholders of the mining industry
