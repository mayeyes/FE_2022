Credible analysis and information feed on the samples such as soil, coal and minerals is required in order to support mine damage prevention, mine development and coal industry. Komir provides professional analysis services with test quality management system based on outstanding analysis technology certified by KOLAS

<Korea Laboratory Accreditation Scheme (KOLAS)>
The Korea Laboratory Accreditation Scheme, or KOLAS, is an official accreditation policy where an appropriate organization under laws or deemed to have international standards evaluate and accredit an agency to have specific testing capabilities and systems required for quality control by conducting a review with professionally certified inspector.
